Vietnamese Fish Sauce (Nuoc Cham)

A quick recipe for Vietnamese dipping sauce

Serving Size: 1 cup

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 0 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons + 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 6 tablespoons hot water
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • carrots
  • 1 fresh birds eye chili pepper

Instructions:

  1. 1. Peel the garlic and finely chop it. Add it to a small bowl.
  2. 2. Cut the lime in half and squeeze all the lime huice into the bowl with the garlic and let it sit for about 10-15 minutes. The acid from the lime juice will cut the sharp garlic spicy bite.
  3. 3. Once 10-15 minutes is up, add the sugar into the garlic/lime juice mix and pour in the hot water. Stire everything together until the sugar dissolves completely.
  4. 4. Once the sugar has dossolved, add in the fish sauce and mix.
  5. 5. Store in refrigerator or use right away.
