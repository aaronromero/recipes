Veal Stock

A French style veal stock.

Serving Size: 4

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 6 hours

Ingredients:

  • 5 pounds veal bones
  • 0.25 cup tomato paste
  • 1 yellow onion, roughly chopped
  • 2 large carrots, roughly chopped
  • 1 celery stalk, including green leaves
  • 1 head of garlic, halved horizontally

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 450F.
  2. Heat a dry roasting pan in the preheated oven for 15 minutes. Add the bones to the hot pan and roast until they are well browned, about 1.5 hours. Use tongs to turn and rotate the bones throughout the cooking time.
  3. When the bones are well browned, add the tomato paste and chopped vegetables. Toss to combine and continue roasting for an additional 30 minutes.
  4. Transfer the contents of the roasting pan to a large stockpot and fill with water (about 8 quarts). Bring to a boil over high heat and skim off any foam that forms on the surface. Lower the heat to maintain a gentle simmer and cook for 5 to 6 hours, skimming fat from the surface every hour or so.
  5. Strain the stock through a fine-mesh sieve. Cool and refrigerate for up to 3 days or freeze for up to 1 month.
