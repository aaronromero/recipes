Wonton Noodle Soup

Its wonton noodle soup

Serving Size: 10

Prep Time: 50 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 36 square wonton wrappers
  • 0.5 pound peeled and deveined shrimp, finely chopped
  • 0.5 pound ground pork or ground chicken
  • 1 tablespoon shaoxing wine or dried sherry
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 0.5 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 teaspoon grated garlic
  • 2 to 3 green onions, finely chopped
  • 0.25 teaspoon sugar
  • 0.25 teaspoon black pepper

For the Noodle Soup:

  • 4 large slices ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 green onions
  • 1 dried shiitake mushroom
  • 5 cups chicken stock
  • 2 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoon fish sauce
  • 2 to 4 heads bok choy or 4 oz of any veggies you like
  • egg noodles

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, add the shrimp, ground pork, 3 cloves of grated garlic, ginger (same amount as garlic), shaoxing wine, soy sauce, sesame oil, black pepper, sugar, cornstarch, and finely chopped green onion. Mix all ingrediants using chopsticks; keep stirring in one direction until everything is mixed.
  2. Make the wontons - add some filling to a wonton wrapper, bring opposite corners together, then fold the ends up. It really doesnt matter as long as its sealed.
  3. In a large stock pot, add 4 slices of ginger, 2 cloves crushed garlic, 2 green onions, shiitake mushroom, 5 cups of chicken stock. Cover with a lid and bring to boil. Let boil for about 5 minutes. Season with 2 teaspoons of soy sauce and fish sauce, each. Pinch of salt and pepper to taste. Remove hard ingredients from the soup.
  4. Keep the soup at a boil, cook the bok choy and plate. Then cook the noodles and plate, Lastly cook the wonton until they rise to the top and plate. If the soup seems reduced add water 1 cup at a time. Add broth to on top of your cooked ingredients and enjoy.
