Mushroom Ravioli

Mushroom ravioli with lemon cream sauce

Serving Size: 4

Prep Time: 90 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Ravioli Filling Ingredients:

  • Homemade pasta dough. See 'Pasta Dough' recipe
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 17.5 oz (500g) Chestnut mushrooms (crimini) sliced
  • 2 shallots finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 sprig thyme
  • 1 heaped tablesp[oon ricotta
  • 2 tablespoon parmesan freshly grated
  • salt and pepper to season

Sauce Ingredients:

  • 1 shallot minced
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1 oz parmesan cheese, grated (1/2 cup), plus extra for serving
  • 2 teaspoons grated lemon zest plus 1/4 cup juice (2 lemons)
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil or parsley

To Make The Filling:

  1. Heat a 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a medium-sized pan, once hot add the finely chopped shallots and saute until soft and translucent.
  2. Add the chopped mushrooms and cook down until reduced in size and softened. Add the garlic and thyme and a good pinch of salt and pepper, fry for 1-2 minutes. Set aside and let cool for 5 minutes.
  3. Add the cooled mushroom mixture to a food processor with parmesan and ricotta. Blitz until smooth and pate like.
  4. Make the ravioli!
  5. Set ravioli aside for meal. You can freeze the rest in a ziplock bag.
  6. Boil water in a pot. Generously salt water. Add ravioli to the water and cook for around 4 minutes. Save 1.5 cups pasta water for sauce.

To Make The Sauce:

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in deep pan over medium heat until shimmering. Add shallot and salt and cook until softened, about 2 minutes. Stir in cream and 1.5 cups reserved cooking water, bring to simmer, and cook for 2 minutes. Off heat, add parmesan, lemon zest and juice, pepper, and remaining 3 tablespoons oil and toss to combine.
  2. Cover and let stand for 2 minutes, tossing frequently and adjusting consistency of sauce with remaining reserved cooking water as needed. Stir in basil and season with salt and papper to taste.
  3. Plate ravioli and top with sauce with extra oil, passing extra parmesan separately.
