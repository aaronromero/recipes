Awasi Dashi

Awase means "combined", so this dashi is made from kombu dashi combined with bonito flakes for an added layer of umami and smoky aftertaste. This is great for miso soup, inarizushi, and oyako don.

Serving Size: 4

Prep Time: 45 minutes

Cook Time: 45 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups Kombu Dashi
  • 1.5 cups (12g) bonito flakes

Instructions:

  1. In a medium pot, bring the kombu dashi to a simmer over medium heat. Turn off the heat and add the bonito flakes to the pot. Turn the heat on low and cook for 3 minutes. Do not stir. Remove the pot from the heat.
  2. Line a fine-mesh sieve with a paper towel, cheasecloth, or clean kitchen towel and set it over a large bowl. Strain the dashi and discard the bonito flakes. Dont wory if there are some flakes in the dashi; they are prefectly edible.
  3. Awase dashi will keep in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days, or frozen in an ice cube tray and stored in a resealable plastic bag for up to 3 months.
