Red Pepper Pasta

Creamy and smokey pasta sauce made from red bell peppers.

Serving Size: 4

Prep Time: 60 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 5 calabrian chilies
  • chili flakes
  • 1 pint heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • Pecorino Romano cheese

Instructions:

  1. Remove the skin from the red bell peppers and slice thin. To remove the skin, a potato peeler works well. Other common practices require roasting the bell pepper and peeling away the skin.
  2. Thinly slice the onions and garlic.
  3. De-stem the Carlabrian chilis and remove the seeds.
  4. Heat olice oil in a pan.
  5. Add the onions, red bell peppers, and a pinch of salt. Cook until softened.
  6. Add the garlic and chilis.
  7. Once everything is cooked down and softened, add tomato paste. Mix in well.
  8. Turn the heat down and add heavy cream. Turn the heat back up and bring to a simmer.
  9. Blend everything to create the sauce. Be careful when blending hot items.
  10. Before serving, heat the sauce in a pan and toss in noodles.
  11. To serve, add Pecorino Romano cheese, black pepper, and cilantro.

Notes:

This pasta is served best with shaped pasta that holds sauce well, such as garganelli.
