Marinated Mushrooms

Button mushrooms marinated in a zesty mixture

Serving Size: 4

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced (or shallot)
  • 1/3 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons dried parsley
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled and crushed
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground dry mustard
  • 1 pound small fresh button mushrooms

Instructions:

  1. Combine onion, vinegar, olive oil, parsley, brwn sugar, garlcic, salt, and dry mustard in a medium saucepan. Bring to a boil. Reduce the heat, stir in mushrooms, and simmer for 10 to 12 minutes, stirring occassionally.
  2. Transfer mushroom mixture to sterile containers and chill in the refrigerator until serving.
