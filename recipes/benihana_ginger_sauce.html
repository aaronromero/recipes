Benihana Ginger Sauce

Serving Size: 12

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 1 minute

Ingredients:

  • 1 yellow onion
  • 2 tbsp fresh ginger
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 3/4 cup soy sauce

Instructions:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a food processor or blender. Pulse/puree until a chunky consistency is achieved. Store in an air-tight container in the refrigerator.
