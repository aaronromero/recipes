Shaking Beef

Description

Serving Size: 4

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 pounds filet mignon, trimmed of excess fat and cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 0.5 cup plus 1 tablspoon canola oil
  • 1 tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 0.25 cup rice vinegar
  • 0.25 cup mirin
  • 0.25 cup light soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon dark soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons fish sauce
  • 1 cup thinly sliced red onion
  • 3 green onions, white and green parts, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 bunch watercress, tough stems removed

Dipping Sauce:

  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 0.25 cup lime juice

Instructions:

  1. 1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the beef, 1 tablespoon of the oil, 1 teaspoon of the sugar, and the salt and pepper. Stire well to combine. Let marinate at room temperature for 2 hours.
  2. 2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the rice vinegar, mirin, ligt and dark soy sauces, fish sauce, and teh remaining tablespoon of sugar until the sugar has dissolved. Set aside.
  3. 3. Heat a wok over high heat until a drop of water evaporates on contact. Add 0.5 cup of the oil and heat until shimmering. Add half of the beef to the wok in a single layer and sear for about 3 minutes, until a brown crust forms on the bottom side. Flip the cubes and cook for 1 minute on the second side. Remove half of the oil and set aside. Add half of the red onions and green onions and cook, stirring, about 30 seconds. Add half the soy sauce mixture and shake the pan to coat the beef. Add half of the garlic and half of the butter and shake the pan to distribute evenly. Trasnfer to a platter and keep warm. Wipe the wok clean, and add the reserved oil, and return it to high heat. Repeat with the remaining half of the ingredients.
  4. 4. To server, arrage the watercress on a platter and top with the beef. To make the dipping sauce, stire together the salt, pepper, and lime juice in a small bowl. Place alongside the platter for dipping the beef.
