Garlicky Chicken Thighs

Description

Serving Size: 2

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 45 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound (500g) skin-on bone-in chichken thighs, trimmed of excess skin and fat
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper
  • 2 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped yellow onion
  • 5 cloves garlic, peeled but left whole
  • 3 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 tablespoons dry white wine (sauvignon blanc, pinot grigio, pinot gris)
  • 1 teaspoon white wine vinegar

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 325F.
  2. Pat the chicken thighs dry and season generously with salt and pepper. In an ovenproof saute pan with a lid over high heat, warm the olive oil. When the oil is hot, add the chicken, skin side down, and sear until golden brown, about 4 minutes. Do not turn. Using tongs, transfer the chicken to paper towels to drain.
  3. Pour off most of the fat from the pan and return it to medium-high heat. Add the onion, garlic, thyme, and bay leaf and saute until the vegetables are just beginning to color, about 4 minutes. Pour in the wine and vinegar and stir with a wooden spoon to dislodhe any browned bits on the pan bottom. Return the chicken thighs, skin side up, to the pan, cover, and place in the oven. Bake until the chicken is very tender, about 40 minutes.
  4. Transfer the thighs to individual plates. Spoon the braising liquid and garlic cloves over the chicken and serve.
